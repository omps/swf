About

Welcome to Sankalp Welfare fondations.

SANKALP is one of the independent non-profitable organization in Mumbai, India established on 8th August 2016 by group of friends having strong passion to bring change in the society, to help poor & needy people and to build better tomorrow. A sole intention of the organization is to bring smile on face of the society and the childrens in particular. We believe every individual has ability to create happiness in the society where we belong to. The organization serves the society with focused objectives and running various projects to achieve the same.

SANKALP gives you a unique opportunity to serve for betterment of society & to boost various educational drives for our better future.

Our main objective and Vision is “Serve for betterment” and be a catalyst to bring in positive changes in the society that we live in. Together we can and together we will.

Invocation for Society and Friends:

Friends, SANKALP is formed by us with a noble intention and we do not know where this path may lead to but we will certainly try to leave a successful trail for others to follow. We are planning to do lots of activities in future initially in and aroundj Thane (Mumbai) area and subsequently the same will be spread to the other area of Maharashtra but our stated objective cannot be achieved or possible without your active guidance and support from friends like you as we strongly believe “coming together is a beginning for us, staying and working together us will be success for SANKALP”.

Friends in life winning isn't everything, but the will to do in order to win the hearts of needy is everything."

We sincerely hope you all will walk with us shoulder to shoulder the challenging journey which SANKALP has just undertaken till it achieve the stated goals.

If you think you are part of this journey we shall really appreciate if you help SANKALP to fulfill its objectives.

To conclude we all know the road of life has always having twists and turns and no two directions are ever the same. Yet our lessons come from the journeys which we have decided to undertake along with you, not the destination.

We hope for your guidance and assistance!!
