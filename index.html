About

Welcome to Sankalp Welfare fondations.

Sankalp Foundation is a non-profitable organisation based in Thane, formed by few friends having strong passion to bring about a change in the lives of under privileged & contribute to the society in general.

To start with Sankalp foundation has adopted a School in Balkum, thane and provided basic amenities like clean drinking water, DeskTop computers, benches etc. and now Sankalp has extended its area of operation with the sole intention of serving the society with focused objective in the rural area schools (Non Govt. grants), which are deprived of basic amenities. We are also identifying rural schools for development which you can find in our upcoming projects.

Our main objective is to 'Serve for Betterment' and to be a catalyst in bringing positive changes in our society. Together we can & together we will.

Invocation

SANKALP is formed by us with a noble intention and we do not know where this path may lead to but we will certainly try to leave a successful trail for others to follow. We are planning to do lots of activities in future initially in and around Thane (Mumbai) and other rural areas of Maharashtra, India. Our stated objective cannot be achieved or possible without your active guidance and support from friends like you as we strongly believe “coming together is a beginning for us, staying and working together us will be success for SANKALP”.

Friends, in life winning isn't everything, but the will to do in order to win the hearts of needy is everything."

We sincerely hope you all will walk with us shoulder to shoulder in this challenging journey which SANKALP has just undertaken till it achieve the stated goals.

If you think you are part of this journey we shall really appreciate if you help SANKALP to fulfill its objectives.

To conclude we all know the road of life is full of twists and turns and no two directions are ever the same. Yet our lessons come from the journeys which we have decided to undertake along with you, not the destination.

We hope for your guidance and assistance!!

Completed project for School Development

SANKALP journey was started in August 2016 and in this small period we have implemented following goals to progress "Serve for betterment" for the needy school.

Balkum - TMC School

Date: Aug 2016

Sankalp Team visited in this school to help and devlope a school where issues were there for water supply and there was no proper water tank for storage, benches were improper and there was no Computer in the school

Sankalp team has done following activities after identify the following areas of improvement:-

  • Provided first Computer in Balkum School.

  • Provided clean drinking water facility by installing water cooler, storage with purifier.

  • Repaired old unusable benches and made one class room with new benches.


Masle-Belpada (Murbad) - Mahatma Phule School

Date: October 2016

SANKALP Team has visited on 1st Oct in rural area School. Its Masle-Belpada area, which is located near Murbad (80KM from Mumbai). The school is located in the middle of 9 villages, where no other school is available for class 8th to 10th. The students come from their village by walk, as there is no transport facility available also main things is, this school doesn't get any Govt. grants.

Sankalp team has done following activities which help and motivate to the children's.

  • Provided school bags to 105 students in this rural area school.

  • Provided Books to students of class 8th, 9th, 10th & make their complete set of books.

  • Provided educational materials for all students, like compass box, study pad etc.

  • Provided motivational & GK books for library at same rural School.

  • Provided 30 benches for class 9th, 10th.

  • Provided laboratory materials for class 9th and 10th.

  • Provided Sports materials.

Reduce Absentism

We would like to work in area of developments which can help school to reduce absenteeism. The SWF Team has done following activity which helps schools and motivate to the students.

  1. Clean drinking water facility for Balkum TMC School

  2. Introduced first computer for Balkum TMC School.

  3. Make one class room with newly benches.

  4. Provided motivational & GK books to Masle-Belpada rural School

  5. Provided sports materials to Masle-Belpada rural School.
